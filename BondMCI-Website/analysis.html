Analysis

On the Songs

Our main focus on analyzing the songs focused on a few things. Firstly was each intro songs use of a keyword. We defined a keyword as the film's title or a variation on the title appearing in the lyrics, a thematic element of 007 appearing in the song's lyrics, or a unique word or reference in the song's lyrics. Secondly we looked at the song's use of perspective and when that switched between first, second, and third person point of view.

On the Scripts

Our focus in the scripts was to record how long speaking parts lasted for male characters vs female characters and tracking that over time.
